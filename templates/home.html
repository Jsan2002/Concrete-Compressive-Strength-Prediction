Project Description

This project aims to predict the compressive strength of concrete based on its components and age. The prediction is made using a machine learning model trained on historical data.

Components

  • Age: The age of the concrete in days
  • Cement: Amount of cement in kg in a m3 mixture
  • Water: Amount of water in kg in a m3 mixture
  • Fly Ash: Amount of fly ash in kg in a m3 mixture
  • Superplasticizer: Amount of superplasticizer in kg in a m3 mixture
  • Blast Furnace Slag: Amount of blast furnace slag in kg in a m3 mixture
